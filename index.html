Trainly

My main project

Trainly is an innovative Android (and soon to be iOS) app transforming how you track train journeys in the UK. Beyond typical offerings of other train apps, Trainly features a live map displaying real-time train locations, which helps as a visual indication of how far away the train is. The app's intuitive design simplifies tracking, offering both diagram and map views. Customizable alerts ensure users receive timely notifications about any delays or cancellations. Trainly is the most reliable solution for stress-free train travel.
