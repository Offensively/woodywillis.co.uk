About me

Hello, I'm Woody, a 15-year-old programming enthusiast from southern England. My passion lies in designing and coding intuitive applications, and I have a soft spot for trains. My coding journey began in 2015 on a flight from Berlin when my father introduced me to Scratch, a simple block coding language, to keep me entertained. Little did I know, this would ignite a fascination with programming.

I eagerly peered over my father's shoulder as he tinkered with PHP, and soon enough, I was diving into coding myself. Most of my programming knowledge has been self-taught since then, a journey fueled by curiosity and a love for problem-solving.

A pivotal experience for me was working with a small yet bustling Minecraft server. The owner generously allowed me to develop for the server, opening doors to the world of version control with git and the importance of code reviews. This exposure not only enhanced my technical skills but also instilled in me the principles of writing clean and readable code.

In essence, my journey in the world of coding has been an exciting adventure so far, from playful beginnings with Scratch to honing my skills on a Minecraft server and now working on my very own train app. I am excited to see where this journey will take me next.
